Our Services

We offer a wide range of services tailored to every pup’s personality and your lifestyle. Whether it’s a quick trim, a weekend retreat, or a ride across town, we’ve got your tail covered.

Grooming

Our grooming sessions are more than just baths—they’re spa days. Your pup will leave clean, trimmed, and camera-ready with premium shampoos and gentle care.
Cost: $25 per session

Boarding

Heading out of town? Let your dog enjoy a mini-vacation in our cozy, supervised boarding suites. Meals, cuddles, and playtime included.
Cost: $50/night

Nutrition

We provide expert dietary plans and handpicked natural food options to keep tails wagging and bellies happy. Customized for every age and breed.
Cost: Consultation from $15

Vet Services

From routine checkups to emergency care, our certified vets offer the best attention your dog can get. Compassionate, experienced, and ready to help.
Cost: $40 per visit

Dog Walking

Busy day? Our walkers give your pup the exercise and attention they crave. Choose from short strolls or long play-run packages.
Cost: $10/walk

Puppy School

Start your pup off right! Our training builds confidence, discipline, and tail-wagging excitement in every lesson.
Cost: $80/month

Adoption Help

Looking to adopt or rehome? We match lovable dogs with their forever humans and guide the process every pawstep of the way.
Cost: Free (Community Service)

Training

Our certified trainers work with dogs of all ages and temperaments using positive reinforcement to build skills that last a lifetime.
Cost: $30/session

Pet Taxi

Need a ride? Our Pet Taxi safely transports your pup to appointments, daycares, or back home after a long adventure.
Cost: $12/trip

Full Membership

All-inclusive access to premium services and VIP experiences, tailored for passionate dog parents who want nothing but the best for their furry friends.

  • Unlimited grooming sessions (includes spa & styling)
  • Priority vet appointments and emergency hotline access
  • Free entry to all DogLover-hosted events & meetups
  • Monthly curated treat & toy delivery box
  • Access to exclusive training workshops and expert webinars
  • Personalized care tracker dashboard and health log
  • Complimentary Pet Taxi rides (up to 4 per month)
Rate: $299/year
Associate Membership

Perfect for light users who still want to enjoy discounted services and stay connected with the Dog Lover community on a flexible plan.

  • Up to 3 grooming sessions per year
  • 10% discount on vet and training services
  • Access to DogLover blog, forum, and basic webinars
  • Monthly newsletter with health tips and events
  • 1 free Pet Taxi ride per year
Fee: 35,000 VND/month

Note: Each membership plan is valid for one dog only. Additional pets will require separate registrations or a family plan upgrade. Service availability may vary by location and appointment scheduling is required. Membership perks cannot be transferred or shared between accounts. Refunds are not provided after the first 7 days of activation.
